Take Over

How to play

A simple conquer the map game. It is a turn based strategy game.

This turn based strategy game is a simple take over the map. Unoccupied territories have barbarians in them at the start of the game. Players start territory are random each game.

Each turn you gain more troops that appear in your home base. You then can move those troops to help foritfy other territories you own. You earn more troops the more territories you control. You gain territories by attacking and defeating all other troops in that territory. You win by defeating the other players home base.
